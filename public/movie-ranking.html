Mike Alan's Top Picks

Top 3 Television Shows of 2023

Barry

A dark character study with biting wit and an unflinching view on violence in Hollywood. Barry, focuses on the world of being a professional assassian, juxtaposed with the humor of being an actor in LA.

Trigun Stampede

Rebooted from the original 90's anime, Trigun Stampede utilizes gorgeous animation and increases the pace of the drama and removes problematic "humor" for the original series.

Mr. Robot

This drama focuses on the double life of Eliot, a hacker and cybersecurity engineer, who struggles with isolation and paranoia. Mr. Robot accurately depicts hacking in a way the very few shows and movies get right.
